FOUNDER PROFILE

Mr. A. Damien Richard

Founder & Managing Director
GKI Events and Travels (OPC) Private Limited

Mr. A. Damien Richard began his career as a freelancer and daily-wage promoter, gaining hands-on experience at the grassroots level of brand promotions and field marketing. Through consistent performance and dedication, he progressed into structured roles within the industry.

Subsequently, due to certain uncertainties and an uncomfortable work environment, he resigned from his position as Director of the organization. With a clear vision and renewed focus, he established his own venture, GKI Events and Travels (OPC) Private Limited, aiming to deliver professional, reliable, and innovative event management and travel solutions across India.

From 2017 onwards, Mr. A. Damien Richard expanded his professional journey by working as a vendor for multiple multinational agencies across India, successfully delivering activation and event projects on a national scale. Building on this experience, he later co-founded a company in partnership under the name Richvin Events and Activations India Private Limited.

A few years later, he joined Wings Brand Activation – Hyderabad Branch, where he served as an Operations Manager for a period of two years. In this role, he was responsible for managing operations, coordinating teams, and executing large-scale brand activation campaigns, significantly strengthening his leadership and operational expertise.
